Game Overview

Mini Golf Championship is a free browser game that brings the classic miniature golf experience directly to your screen. This online mini golf game features 18 meticulously designed courses filled with creative obstacles, ramps, and hazards that challenge players of all skill levels. Perfect for casual gamers and golf enthusiasts alike, the game combines easy-to-learn controls with progressively challenging gameplay that keeps you coming back for more.

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming break or want to master every course, this free-to-play mini golf experience offers hours of entertainment without any downloads or installations required. Simply open your web browser and start putting your way through beautifully crafted holes with realistic physics and smooth controls.

How to Play Mini Golf Championship

Use your mouse to aim your shot by pointing in the direction you want the ball to go. Click and drag backwards to set the power - the further you drag, the stronger your shot will be. Watch out for obstacles, slopes, and water hazards that can affect your ball's path. Try to complete each hole in par or better!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this mini golf game completely free to play?

Yes! This online mini golf game is completely free to play with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. Simply visit our website and start playing immediately in your web browser.

No downloads or installations required! This is a browser-based game that runs directly in your web browser. It's compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge on both desktop and mobile devices.

Use your mouse to aim by pointing in the direction you want the ball to go. Click and drag backwards to set the power - the further you drag, the stronger your shot will be. On mobile devices, simply touch and drag to aim and shoot.
